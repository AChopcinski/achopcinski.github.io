About Me

Here's the zones where I see each bowling ball I have in rotation for leagues/touranments currently. The zones are where I feel comfortable and knowing they can strike semi-consistently from. They can strike from anywhere obviously but where they are most likely to for me.

The Benchmarks

Optimum Idol and Theorem

The Idol is the first ball I use everywhere. It's strong, and I have no issue playing 21-12 with it, however I find it starts to really work well 24-12 out to like 45-24. The Theorem needs to hit dry, but it's a tank. It can carry when missing left but more often than not I'll leave a 2 pin. These work well off of each other for me.

TroubleMaker Pearl and Brutal Collision

I like both of these, but I don't use them often. The Brutal Collision and Idol are fairly close for me so it's a matter of preference and I bring the Idol everywhere since it has a wider area to play with. The Troublemaker is good-ish, it's just, I dunno, it looks either good or bad no middle ground.

The Beast Hybrid and Nu Blue Hammer

The Beast Hybrid I can use way outside for a few frames, and then way inside at the end of the night, very rarely in between. The Nu Blue is comfortable in a small urethane-like zone for me. It gets squirrelly going too much further left without surface. Using it at 500 mmoves the left boundary of the zone 5 and 5 left.

Purple Hammer and Motiv Tank

On cheetah, this is about how each ball was playing. While I know the purple was reading roughly the same spot as the Tank, 5 feet in front of the foul line, it was holding it's line considerably longer before really flipping.
